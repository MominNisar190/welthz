# How to Use Manual Monthly Report Feature

## Quick Guide

### Step 1: Go to Dashboard
Navigate to your dashboard at `/dashboard`

### Step 2: Scroll to Bottom
Scroll down past your accounts to find the "Send Monthly Report" card

### Step 3: Select Month
Click the dropdown and choose any month from the last 12 months:
- March 2026
- February 2026
- January 2026
- December 2025
- ... and so on

### Step 4: Send Report
Click the "Send Report to Email" button

### Step 5: Wait
The system will:
1. Gather your transactions for that month
2. Calculate income and expenses
3. Generate AI insights using Gemini
4. Send email via Resend
5. Show success message

### Step 6: Check Email
Open your email inbox and look for:
- **From**: Finance App <onboarding@resend.dev>
- **Subject**: Your Financial Report - [Month Name]

## Tips

### Best Practices
✅ Send reports for months with transactions
✅ Review AI insights for spending tips
✅ Compare multiple months to see trends
✅ Save emails for record keeping

### What to Avoid
❌ Don't send reports for months with no transactions
❌ Don't spam the send button (wait for completion)
❌ Don't expect instant delivery (takes 3-8 seconds)

## Troubleshooting

### "No transactions found for [Month]"
**Problem**: Selected month has no transactions
**Solution**: Choose a different month with transactions

### "Failed to send report"
**Problem**: Network or server error
**Solution**: 
1. Check internet connection
2. Wait a moment and try again
3. Refresh the page if needed

### Email not received
**Problem**: Email not in inbox
**Solution**:
1. Check spam/junk folder
2. Wait a few minutes (can take up to 5 minutes)
3. Verify your email address in account settings
4. Try sending again

### Loading forever
**Problem**: Button stuck in loading state
**Solution**:
1. Refresh the page
2. Try again
3. Check browser console for errors

## Comparison: Manual vs Automatic Reports

| Feature | Manual Report | Automatic Report |
|---------|--------------|------------------|
| **Timing** | On-demand | 1st of each month |
| **Month** | Any past 12 months | Previous month only |
| **Trigger** | User clicks button | Automated by system |
| **Frequency** | Unlimited | Once per month |
| **Use Case** | Review past data | Regular updates |

## FAQ

### Q: How many reports can I send?
**A**: Unlimited! Send as many as you need.

### Q: Can I send reports for future months?
**A**: No, only past 12 months are available.

### Q: Will I be charged for sending reports?
**A**: No, it's completely free (within Resend's free tier limits).

### Q: Can I send reports to someone else?
**A**: Currently, reports are sent to your registered email only.

### Q: How long does it take?
**A**: Usually 3-8 seconds to generate and send.

### Q: What if I have no transactions?
**A**: You'll see an error message. Select a month with transactions.

### Q: Can I customize the report?
**A**: Not yet, but this could be a future feature!

### Q: Are AI insights accurate?
**A**: They're generated by Gemini AI based on your actual spending data.
